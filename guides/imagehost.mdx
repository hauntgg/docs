## How to View Your Image Host Statistics

<Steps>
    <Step title="Open Your Image Host Overview">
        Open your overview on https://haunt.gg/dashboard/imagehost/overview, once you're on that page, you will need to navigate to the top of the bage where you'll see your statistics.
    </Step>
    <Step title="View Your Uplaod Analytics"
        Under the "upload analytics", you can view your total upload analytics throughout the past 24 Hours, Seven Days, 14 Days, or 30 days.
    </Step>
    <Step title="File Types"
        View your most common file types based on your previous uploads. If you have no uploads, it'll say "no file type data available".
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
    To expand your storage to 10GB, purchase premium today. 
</Note>

## General Customization

<Steps>
    <Step title="Open the Section"
        Go to the **General Customization** section.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Customize Your Image Host Settings"
        Once you're in the General Customization section, you can start customizing your settings to incoprorate your personal desires!
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Embed Settings

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to the Section"
        Navigate to the **Embed Settings** section.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Customize Your Embed"
        Customize your embed accordingly to your preferences. Once you've customized it, ensure that you save all your changes so your future embeds are updated!
    </Steps>

<Note>
    Your timestamp will appear as a footer on the embed. Timestamps automatically sync to anyone viewing it's local tiimezone or set timezone on their device.
</Note>

## Uploading an Image 

<Steps>
    <Step title="Begin Uploading a File"
        Click the "Click to Upload a File" button.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Choose Your File"
        Browse through your file explorer until you find the file you'd like to upload.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Copy Your Link"
        Copy your link and then send it on your desired communication platform
    </Step>

<Warning>
    If your out of file space, head to your gallery to delete files and then upload your file.
</Warning>

## Gallery

<Steps>
    <Step title="Search Through Your Files"
        To find your file, search through your gallery using the name of the file.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Filter Through The Gallery"
        If you'd like to find your file based on date or file size, you can click on the filter button to sift through them according to your selection.
    </Step>
